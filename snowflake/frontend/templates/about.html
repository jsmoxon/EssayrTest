About


Essayer is the French word for “to try”. That's what this site is: an attempt.

It's an attempt to create a space where people can be honest and open about topics that are important to them.

The format is Question & Answer in the belief that it encourages new ways of seeing the world. Maybe someone will phrase a question in a way that you never could have and you can answer it in a way they hadn't imagined.

The hope is that by allowing users to share their stories and thoughts in a place that is clutter free and simple, we’ll create a mosaic of essays and stories that open up some interesting avenues of thought.

The only thing we ask is that you approach Essayr earnestly.

The project is inspired by my friends' experiences with writing and this essay by Paul Graham.
